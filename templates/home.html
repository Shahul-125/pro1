Our Services

We provide guaranteed admissions to top colleges across India, including prominent institutions in Tamil Nadu and other states. Every year, we see many bright students from our region unable to pursue higher education despite scoring excellent marks, due to a lack of guidance or financial support. Our mission is to ensure that no deserving student misses out on a college education. we also help students obtain scholarships and education loans to ease their financial burden. For students aspiring to study abroad, we offer full support in securing seats in well-ranked international universities in the country of their choice. We also provide free assistance with Government Counselling procedures, including Choice Filling, ensuring that students don’t miss out on valuable opportunities due to lack of information or technical challenges. Our services are completely free of cost. There are no hidden charges or consultancy fees. Students are encouraged to pay their tuition fees directly to the colleges, ensuring transparency at every step. With our dedicated support, you or your child can step confidently into a bright academic future. Let us guide you toward the best possible education opportunities in India or abroad.

About Us

PSS Educational Consultancy is a trusted educational consultancy committed to helping students achieve their academic goals. We provide expert guidance for admissions into top universities in India, such as SRM, Sathyabama, LPU, Parul University, and more. Our services include free assistance with government counselling (including choice filling), securing scholarships, and education loan support. For students aspiring to study abroad, we offer end-to-end admission support to reputed international institutions. We operate with complete transparency—no consultancy fees or hidden charges. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to all deserving students by providing personalized support every step of the way.
